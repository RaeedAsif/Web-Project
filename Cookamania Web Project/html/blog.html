Trieste - A new addition in twin cities

Trieste is a recently opened restaurant in Bahria Town, Phase 7. I went there expecting an Italian restaurant but I was wrong. The menu is eclectic and includes dishes from all over the world, including Italian of course. The restaurant is nicely decorated, and has two floors. There was no one to greet customers on the ground floor so we climbed upstairs to find the very courteous manager who showed us to our tables. We ordered nachos as appetizer and I ordered Pepper steak as main course. Rate: 7.5 / 10.
